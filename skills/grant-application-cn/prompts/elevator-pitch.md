# Prompt: 30 秒电梯演讲（Elevator Pitch）模板

## 用途

帮助用户撰写**30 秒可口语化、5 句话、180-220 词左右**的研究项目电梯演讲（elevator pitch）。这一段在多个场景反复出现：

- **NIH Specific Aims 第 1 段**（Hook + Gap + Long-term goal）
- **NSF Project Summary Overview**（1 段说清研究 + objectives + methods）
- **ERC Extended Synopsis 第 1 段**（state of the art + question + ambition）
- **MSCA PF Excellence 第 1 段**（state of the art + objectives）
- **NIH Biographical Sketch Personal Statement 第 1 段**（why uniquely qualified）
- **国家社科基金立项依据第 1 段**（背景 + 问题 + 价值）
- **学术汇报 / Tenure 答辩 / Job market talk 开场白**
- **与 PO / panel chair / collaborator 的非正式 1-on-1 introduction**

> **核心认识**：好的 pitch 不是"摘要的更短版本"，是**一个独立的故事**——3 句 setup + 1 句 turn + 1 句 stakes。

## 5 句黄金结构

```
Sentence 1 — SETUP (Big picture + tension)
[领域 / 现象] [一个 well-accepted 但即将被挑战的事实 / 数据]

Sentence 2 — KNOWN (What we already know)
[已有共识 + 已有方法]，引 1-2 个标志性工作

Sentence 3 — UNKNOWN / GAP (Where the puzzle starts)
"However, [关键的 missing piece]——具体到一个机制 / 数字 / 现象"

Sentence 4 — TURN (Your move)
"In this project, we [具体动词] [具体方法] to [具体目标]."

Sentence 5 — STAKES (Why it matters if you succeed)
[一句话答 "so what" — 学术 / 临床 / 政策 / 产业]
```

**目标**：朗读 30 秒（约 75-90 词英文 / 200-240 字中文）。

## 项目体例特化

### 模式 A: NIH 临床 / 机制导向（适用 R01 Specific Aims Para 1）

```
[1] Despite [decades of advance / billions in investment], [clinical outcome] 
remains a major problem, with [specific number].
[2] Tumor-intrinsic / Pathway-level / Genetic features X, Y, Z have been 
implicated [Smith 2024], yet [Z] remains uncharacterized.
[3] *This represents a critical barrier to [therapeutic / preventive] progress.*
[4] In this project, we will determine how [Z] regulates [Y], leveraging our 
[unique cohort / PDX panel / pilot data].
[5] Successful completion will identify a new resistance axis and provide 
preclinical rationale for [combination therapy / biomarker stratification].
```

**具体 worked example**:

> *Despite immune checkpoint inhibitors revolutionizing oncology, fewer than 
> 25% of metastatic triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC) patients achieve 
> durable response [Mittendorf 2024]. Tumor-intrinsic features X, Y have 
> been implicated, yet the role of [factor A] remains uncharacterized — 
> a critical barrier to next-generation immunotherapy. In this project, we 
> will determine how [A] suppresses CD8+ T cell infiltration in TNBC, 
> leveraging our 12-PDX panel and preliminary CRISPR-screen data. Successful 
> completion will define a new resistance axis and provide preclinical 
> rationale for [A]-blockade + αPD-1 combination trials within 5 years.*

### 模式 B: NSF 基础科学 / 工程导向（适用 Project Summary Overview）

```
[1] [Field] has long assumed that [prevailing assumption].
[2] Recent advances in [enabling technology / theory] reveal that this 
assumption may not hold under [specific conditions].
[3] We propose to test [reframed hypothesis] by [specific empirical / 
theoretical strategy].
[4] If successful, this work will [reframe the field / enable a new 
class of methods / answer a longstanding open question].
[5] Broader impacts include [education / DEI / translation / infra]: 
[1 specific deliverable].
```

### 模式 C: ERC ground-breaking 导向（适用 Extended Synopsis 第 1 段）

```
[1] Current [field] research treats [phenomenon X] and [phenomenon Y] 
as separate.
[2] This separation is a historical artifact, not a scientific necessity.
[3] In this project, we will develop [unified framework / new method / 
integrative theory] that bridges X and Y.
[4] If successful, this opens a new line of inquiry impossible under the 
current paradigm — high-risk because [why hard] but high-gain because 
[transformative outcome].
[5] My laboratory is uniquely positioned to lead this effort, with [PI's 
key prior breakthroughs in both X and Y].
```

### 模式 D: 国家社科基金 / 教育部立项依据首段

```
[1] [宏观背景一句，连接国家战略 / 重大现实问题]。
[2] 学界普遍认为 [当前共识]（参见 [代表学者] 等近年研究）。
[3] 然而，[关键不足或矛盾]——[核心研究问题以问句方式直接抛出]？
[4] 本研究拟采用 [理论框架 + 方法] 系统回答这一问题，重点突破 
[2-3 个具体方面]。
[5] 这一研究不仅关乎 [理论建构]，更对 [国家战略 / 政策实践] 具有 
直接的 [量化承诺]。
```

**具体 worked example**:

> *新质生产力的提出标志着中国高质量发展进入新阶段（习近平，2024）。学界普遍
> 认为新质生产力以"技术革命性突破 + 生产要素创新性配置 + 产业深度转型升级"
> 为核心特征（任保平，2024）。然而，**新质生产力的形成机制、跨区域测度方法
> 及其对区域经济差距的影响**尚未系统厘清——新质生产力究竟通过何种机制重构
> 区域经济格局？本研究拟采用"内生增长理论 + 空间计量模型 + 多源大数据"系统
> 回答这一问题，重点突破：(1) 构建多维度新质生产力测度体系；(2) 识别新质
> 生产力影响区域差距的因果机制；(3) 提出区域协同发展的政策路径。这一研究
> 不仅关乎"新发展阶段"理论建构，更直接服务于"区域协调发展"和"共同富裕"
> 战略，预期形成 1 份政策报告报送国家发展改革委。*

### 模式 E: NIH Personal Statement 第 1 段 ("PI elevator pitch")

```
[1] I am a [职务] at [机构] with [N] years of expertise in 
[研究领域 + sub-area].
[2] My laboratory has [unique capability — cohort / model / method / 
dataset] and has published [N] papers establishing [foundational 
contribution] in this area.
[3] I am uniquely positioned to lead this proposal because [3 specific 
elements: preliminary data / unique resource / track record / 
collaboration network].
```

注：PI Personal Statement 的电梯演讲是 **3 句**而非 5 句，因为后面还有"Most relevant contributions"段补足。

## 写作原则

### 原则 1: 一句一职责，绝不复合

每句承担**唯一**功能：背景 / 已知 / 未知 / 你的动作 / 影响。把 2 个功能挤进一句话 = reviewer 理解失焦。

### 原则 2: 每句必须能"单独读懂"

写完后**只读其中 1 句**——它能不能 stand alone？如果第 3 句"However the gap is..."脱离上下文不知道 gap 是什么，重写。

### 原则 3: Specificity beats elegance

具体数字 / 具体方法 / 具体作者 > 优美抽象。reviewer 看到 "fewer than 25%" 比 "limited efficacy" 印象深。

### 原则 4: 第 4 句（Turn）的动词要"硬"

弱：study, investigate, examine, explore（reviewer：what does this even mean?）

强：determine, test, identify, define, quantify, characterize, develop, derive, establish

### 原则 5: 第 5 句（Stakes）必须"领域级"，不是"项目级"

弱（项目级）：「we will publish in top journals」
强（领域级）：「will reframe how the field thinks about [phenomenon]」/「will provide preclinical rationale for [trial]」/「为政策制定提供 [具体依据]」

## 反例分析

### 反例 1: 流水账

> *Cancer is a leading cause of death. Many studies have looked at this. 
> We propose to study cancer mechanisms. We will use new methods. This 
> work is important.*

**问题**：5 句话 0 信息量。每句都可以替换成任何其他领域而不需修改。

### 反例 2: 长句堆砌

> *Despite numerous advances in our understanding of the molecular and 
> cellular mechanisms underlying immunotherapy resistance in triple-negative 
> breast cancer over the past several decades, including the identification 
> of key regulatory pathways such as ..., the precise role of ...*

**问题**：第 1 句就 60 词。reviewer 读到"despite"已经走神。**砍成 2-3 句**。

### 反例 3: 缺 Turn

> *X is important. Many people study X. The gap is big. This is important.*

**问题**：5 句中 4 句在 setup，没有"我做什么" Turn 句。reviewer 读完不知道你在提议什么。

### 反例 4: 只有 Turn 没有 Setup

> *We propose to use single-cell RNA-seq + CRISPR screens + spatial 
> transcriptomics to identify novel therapeutic targets in TNBC.*

**问题**：reviewer 不知道**为什么需要识别 TNBC 的 targets**——直接陷入方法堆砌。

## 工作流

1. 询问用户：项目类型 + 研究问题 + 已知 / 未知 / 拟方法 / 影响
2. 选择最契合的模式（A/B/C/D/E）
3. 按 5 句模板填空，强制每句单一职责
4. **朗读测试**：读 30 秒能不能读完？读完能不能让一个非领域同事复述大意？
5. **3 次重写**，每次砍 10-20%（强 pitch 的最后一稿通常比初稿短 25%）
6. 自检：5 个原则逐条检查

## 5 句自检 Checklist

- [ ] 第 1 句**有 tension**（surprise / contradiction / specific number）？不是"X is important"？
- [ ] 第 2 句**引 1-2 个具名工作**？不是"many studies have shown"？
- [ ] 第 3 句**显式标记 gap**（用"However" / "Yet" / "然而"）？gap 具体到机制 / 数字 / 现象？
- [ ] 第 4 句**有硬动词**（determine / test / identify ...）+ **具体方法**？
- [ ] 第 5 句**领域级 stakes**，不是项目级"we will publish"？
- [ ] 总长度：英文 75-90 词 / 中文 200-240 字（30 秒朗读）？
- [ ] 朗读时**无需任何幻灯片**就能让人理解？
