# NSF: Intellectual Merit + Broader Impacts (Merit Review 2.0)

NSF 所有 proposal 必须明确回答两大评分维度：**Intellectual Merit (IM)** 和 **Broader Impacts (BI)**。两者**缺一不可**——单独把某一项写得很强但另一项偏弱仍会被退稿（reviewer 必须给两项分别评级，任一项弱整本就不可能 "Highly Competitive"）。

> 适用范围：本文针对 NSF 通用 Merit Review（PAPPG 24-1 现行版）+ CAREER 特别要求。其他 directorate 项目（如 CISE Medium、BIO 的特殊机制）通常都建立在这套框架上。

## 1. NSF Merit Review Criteria（PAPPG 24-1）

每位 reviewer 必须按 IM + BI 两项分别打分（rating: Excellent / Very Good / Good / Fair / Poor）。**Project Summary 和 Project Description 中都需要明确分两个 subsection** 标注。

### 1.1 Intellectual Merit — 评审会逐条问的 5 个问题

> "The Intellectual Merit criterion encompasses the potential to advance knowledge."

NSF reviewer 收到的 evaluation form 上列出（PAPPG II.D.2）：

1. What is the potential for the proposed activity to:
   (a) Advance knowledge and understanding within its own field?
   (b) Advance knowledge across different fields?
2. To what extent do the proposed activities suggest and explore **creative, original, or potentially transformative concepts**?
3. Is the plan **well-reasoned, well-organized, and based on a sound rationale**?
4. How well **qualified** is the individual, team, or organization to conduct the proposed activities?
5. Are there **adequate resources** available to the PI (either at the home organization or through collaborations)?

→ Project Description 中 IM 段落必须能 1-1 对应回答这 5 项；最好有 sub-headers 让 reviewer 5 秒内定位。

### 1.2 Broader Impacts — NSF 列出的可计入类别

> "The Broader Impacts criterion encompasses the potential to benefit society and contribute to the achievement of specific, desired societal outcomes."

PAPPG 列出的可计入 outcomes（**5 大类**——下文 §3 展开"strong examples"）：

1. **Full participation** of women, persons with disabilities, and underrepresented minorities in STEM
2. **Improved STEM education and educator development** at any level
3. **Increased public scientific literacy** and public engagement with science and technology
4. **Improved well-being of individuals in society**
5. **Development of a diverse, globally competitive STEM workforce**
6. **Increased partnerships** between academia, industry, and others
7. **Improved national security**
8. **Increased economic competitiveness** of the United States
9. **Enhanced infrastructure for research and education**

> **核心要求**：必须把 broader impacts 落实到**具体活动 + 受益人群 + 渠道 + 评估指标**。空泛声明（"我们将提升 underrepresented groups 的参与"）会被 reviewer 直接打 Fair 或 Poor。

## 2. Project Summary 的 1 页 3-段式（必须）

NSF Project Summary（限 **1 页**）要求三个 separate sections（**必须分别加 header**）：

```
Overview
[~ 1/3 页] 用一段话描述：本提案的研究内容、objectives、methods. 
对所在领域科学家通俗，但又能在更广受众中被理解.

Intellectual Merit
[~ 1/3 页] 显式回答：本工作如何 advance knowledge in/across the field?
What is creative / original / transformative? Why is the team qualified?

Broader Impacts
[~ 1/3 页] 显式声明：受益人群是谁？通过什么 channel？metric 是什么？
Sustainability + evaluation 短句各 1 句.
```

> **任一 section 缺失 / 缺 header / 全在一段 → "without review" 直接退回 (return without review)**。这是 NSF 最严格的格式 trigger 之一。FastLane / Research.gov 系统会做基本格式校验，但缺 header 不一定被系统自动捕获——退回是 PO 人工审查的结果。

## 3. Broader Impacts 写作模板 + 5 类强示例

### 3.1 为什么 BI 容易被 dismissed

NSF panel 文献和 reviewer 反馈反复指出 BI 部分**最常见的 4 大败笔**：

1. **"Generic and expected of any PI"**：例如"我会 mentor graduate students" / "我会发表论文"——这些是 PI 的本职，不算 BI。
2. **"Overreaching without specifics"**：例如"will impact K-12 education statewide" 但没具体合作学校 / 课程 / 学生数 / 评估方式。
3. **"Reinventing the wheel"**：又一个项目网站、又一篇科普 blog——除非有独特功能。
4. **"Two parallel lines that never connect"**：BI 与 research 完全脱节，让 panel 觉得 "tacked on at the end"。

→ **强 BI 的核心特征：BI 与 research 同源（research outputs feed BI activities，BI activities generate research data / participants）；具体（数字 + 合作单位 + 评估）；可持续（项目结束后谁接盘）**。

### 3.2 5 类 strong examples（按 NSF 公开的获奖案例归纳）

#### 类 1: 教育（K-12 / undergraduate / graduate）

> **Strong**: "We will partner with [具名 Title-I high school district]. The PI's lab will train 4 high school teachers per summer (years 2-5) in [research topic]; teachers will deploy a 2-week curriculum module to ~600 students/yr. Pre/post Likert + concept-inventory assessment will be analyzed by external evaluator [name, institution]. Curricula will be hosted on QUBES / TeachEngineering for sustainability."
>
> **Weak**: "PI will engage with K-12 schools to promote STEM."

#### 类 2: 欠 represented groups (DEI / participation)

> **Strong**: "Through partnership with [HBCU / HSI / TCU name], we will fund 2 REU students/yr from underrepresented groups in computing (matched on department demographics; tracked 5 yr post-program for graduate enrollment). Mentor-mentee structure follows the Entering Mentoring framework. Outcomes: # of REU students continuing to PhD; year-over-year participation rate."
>
> **Weak**: "Our lab welcomes diversity and we will prioritize underrepresented students in our hiring."

#### 类 3: Society / public well-being / SOC (Societal Outcomes Communication)

> **Strong**: "Working with [community partner / city department], we will integrate research outputs into [policy decision support tool / community resource]. The tool will be co-designed with [stakeholder group, n = X], with iterative usability testing in years 2 and 4. Adoption metrics: # of municipal users; # of decisions informed."
>
> **Weak**: "Our work has the potential to improve health outcomes for the public."

#### 类 4: Policy / Industry / Workforce

> **Strong**: "Collaborator [industry firm name] commits to host 1 PhD intern/yr to translate Aim 2 outputs into a [specific product feature]. Joint patents and a workforce-aligned curricular module (delivered via [extension / community college partner]) target 200 incumbent workers/yr in [region with relevant industry concentration]."
>
> **Weak**: "Outcomes will be relevant to industry."

#### 类 5: Infrastructure / Open Science / Data Sharing

> **Strong**: "All software developed will be released under MIT license on GitHub with versioned DOIs (Zenodo). The dataset (~5 TB processed) will be archived on [domain repository, e.g., Dryad / OpenNeuro / NCBI]. We commit to ≥ 5-yr maintenance via [institutional / consortium] funding. Estimated reach: ~500 downloads/yr based on similar resources from PI's prior NSF awards."
>
> **Weak**: "Code and data will be available upon request."

### 3.3 Broader Impacts section 推荐模板（Project Description 内）

```
Broader Impacts

[Anchor sentence: BI 的总命题，1 句]

Specific activities:

(1) [Activity name] — Education / DEI / SOC / Industry / Infra
    - 受益人群: [who, n]
    - 合作渠道: [partner names + signed letters]
    - 时间表: [year by year]
    - 评估指标: [internal + external; formative + summative]

(2) ... (建议 2-4 项 well-resourced 的活动；不要 8 项浅活动)

Integration with Research:
[1 段说明 BI 活动如何与 research 互相 feed——展示二者不是平行而是同源]

Sustainability:
[1 段说明项目结束后哪些活动可持续？谁接盘？经费来源？]

Evaluation:
- Internal evaluation: [PI + co-PI 跟踪 metric]
- External evaluation: [name + institution + role]
- Formative (mid-project) + summative (year 5) reports
```

### 3.4 NSF 2023+ 的 trend: "specific, desired societal outcomes"

PAPPG 24-1 起 NSF 强调 BI 必须连接到**"specific, desired societal outcomes"**——不能再用"will improve education"这类宽泛短语。每个 BI activity 必须能回答：
- **谁**（具体人群、最好有 demographics）
- **多少**（n + 频率）
- **如何衡量**（指标 + 评估者）
- **何时可见效果**（formative + summative timeline）

## 4. CAREER 项目特别要求 — Integrated Research-Education Plan

NSF CAREER 是青年学者最高荣誉级 grant。Project Description（**15 pages**）必须含 **integrated research and education plan**——这是 CAREER 与 Standard Grant 最大区别。

### 4.1 5-year structure

CAREER 资助 5 年。Project Description 推荐结构（公开 winning proposals 的共性）：

```
A. Introduction & Career Goals (1 page)
   - PI 的 5 年 career vision（不是 5 年研究计划——而是 PI 自己 want to become 什么样的 scholar/educator）
   - Research + Education 如何 integrated 的 high-level statement

B. Research Plan (8-9 pages)
   - Specific Aims (~3 个)
   - Each aim: rationale + design + preliminary results + expected outcomes + risk
   - 必须显式标 "Intellectual Merit" subsection 或在每个 aim 下含 IM bullet

C. Education Plan (4-5 pages) — 不能是后想到的附加！
   - C1. Curriculum development（新课 / 改课）
   - C2. Student training pipeline（K-12 outreach / undergrad / grad）
   - C3. Department-level integration（与院系 strategic plan 的契合）
   - C4. Evaluation plan
   - 每项含: 受众 / 合作伙伴 / 时间表 / 指标

D. Integration of Research and Education (~1 page) — KEY
   - Research → Education flow: 数据 / case study / 工具如何进入课堂
   - Education → Research flow: 学生项目 / 课程产出如何 inform research
   - 必须给出 ≥ 2 项 concrete bidirectional links（不只是 "students will work in my lab"）

E. Broader Impacts (≤ 1 page or integrated above)

F. Results from Prior NSF Support (if any) (1 page max)
```

### 4.2 Department Chair Letter

CAREER 必须附 **Department Chair Letter**（不计页限）——明确支持 PI 的 education plan + 描述 department 如何提供 release time / 教学资源。**Letter 模板化（chair 一年签 5 封同样的）会被 reviewer 看出**，需要 PI 主动给 chair 提供 specific paragraphs。

### 4.3 Budget 注意

- 总预算 ≥ $400K（除 BIO/PLR 是 $500K），5 年总额
- 必须含 1 month summer salary × 5 yr（让 PI 有专注时间）
- Education plan 的费用（材料 / outreach travel / external evaluator）必须清晰列出

## 5. NSF vs NIH 体例对照

| 维度 | NSF | NIH |
|------|-----|-----|
| 强制 review criteria | Intellectual Merit + Broader Impacts | Significance / Investigator / Innovation / Approach / Environment（2025 起 3 Factor） |
| 1 页摘要 | Project Summary（**3 sections** required headers） | Specific Aims（5 段式，无 header 要求） |
| 主体长度 | Project Description 15 pages | Research Strategy 12 pages（不含 Specific Aims） |
| 假设驱动？ | 偏好但不强制 | 几乎强制 hypothesis-driven |
| Preliminary data 必需？ | 不强制（CAREER 强烈鼓励） | 强烈建议（R01/K，R21 例外） |
| Resubmission 制度 | 不允许直接 resubmit；同主题再申请算"new submission" | 可 resubmit 1 次（A1） |
| 评审流程 | Panel + ad hoc reviewer 混合 | Study section 集中 panel |
| 数值评分？ | 无 priority score；rating + ranking | 1-9 + impact score 10-90 + percentile |
| Letters of Collaboration | **必须**简短"I confirm I'll do X"风格——禁止评价/推荐内容 | Letters of Support 可含 evaluative content |

## 6. 评审流程 — Panel + Ad Hoc 混合

NSF 通常采用 **panel + ad hoc** 混合（具体因 program 而异）：

1. **Program Officer (PO)** 接收 proposal，分配到合适 program
2. **Ad hoc reviewer**：3-5 位 written reviews（远程；通常 2-4 周内）
3. **Panel meeting**：~15 位 panelist 集中 1-2 天讨论一批 proposals
4. **Panel ranking**：通常打 5 档 — Highly Competitive / Competitive / Recommended / Low Competitive / Not Competitive
5. **PO 综合**：ad hoc + panel + program priority + budget → recommendation
6. **NSF Division** 最终决定 → award / decline

### 6.1 PO 角色（中国学者常忽略的关键）

无 NIH 式 priority score；funding decision 含 **PO 主观裁量空间**。因此：

- **Pre-submission**：邮件 PO 1 页 concept paper（"Is this a fit for your program?"）通常会回复
- **Post-decline**：可申请 PO 1-on-1 phone call 听取意见，对 resubmit 极有帮助
- **PO 的 program priority** 每年微调（如某 division 当年 emphasize quantum / AI for science）—— 与时俱进调整 framing

## 7. Data Management & Sharing Plan

NSF 自 2011 起强制要求 **Data Management Plan (DMP)**，**最多 2 页**，单独附件。包含：

1. **Types of data** / samples / physical collections / software / curriculum materials
2. **Data formats**（preferred: 开放、文档化的格式）
3. **Access policies**（embargo? license?）
4. **Preservation strategy**（哪个 repository, 多少年, DOI 安排）
5. **Roles and responsibilities**（谁负责 curation）

**2024 起 trend**：NSF Public Access Plan 要求所有 NSF-funded data 默认开放，需在 DMP 中说明开放时间与例外。

## 8. 高频退稿原因（以 reviewer 反馈样本归纳）

1. **Broader Impacts 空泛**：写"我们将增加 underrepresented groups 的参与"但无 specific 合作 / 指标 / 评估
2. **Intellectual Merit 与 Broader Impacts 混淆**：把"将产出更多论文"当 broader impact
3. **Project Summary 缺三个 header** → return without review
4. **CAREER 的 education plan 写得像后想到的附加** → "lacks integration"
5. **DMP 缺失或敷衍**（< 1 页且只写"数据将公开"）
6. **Budget 与 scope 不符**：如人员月数 / 设备费配置不合理；CAREER 总额低于 floor
7. **Letters of Collaboration 用错语言**：必须是简短"I confirm I'll do X"风格，**严禁**含 evaluation / recommendation 内容（PAPPG 明确禁止）—— 含评价性语言会触发 return without review
8. **Project Description 没有 Broader Impacts 单独 subsection**（PAPPG 要求 BI 在 Project Description 内显式标头）
9. **Results from Prior NSF Support** 缺失（5 年内有 NSF 资助必须列）

## 9. 中国 PI 申请 NSF 的现实

- NSF 限 **PI 在美机构**（U.S. accredited institutions of higher education / nonprofit research orgs）
- 中国 PI 通常作为 **Subaward to U.S. PI** 或 **collaborator on letter**（不参与预算）
- 跨境合作要遵守 **CHIPS and Science Act 2022** 之后的 NSF 安全条款（specific collaborator 国别披露）
- **TRUST: TRusted USer Sponsored research** 系统下，"foreign affiliations" 必须在 Senior Personnel 文件中披露所有 ≥ 12 个月的境外 affiliation

## 10. 自检 Checklist

- [ ] Project Summary 1 页内 + 3 个 header（Overview / Intellectual Merit / Broader Impacts）
- [ ] Project Description 显式分 IM 与 BI subsections？
- [ ] BI 含 specific 受益人群 + 合作单位 + 评估指标 + sustainability？
- [ ] DMP 单独 ≤ 2 页 + 含 5 项要素？
- [ ] CAREER 含 5 年 career goals + integrated R&E plan + dept chair letter？
- [ ] Letters of Collaboration **不含**评价性内容？
- [ ] Results from Prior NSF Support（5 年内 PI 主持过 NSF）已列？
- [ ] Senior Personnel 文件含所有 ≥ 12 个月境外 affiliation 披露？
- [ ] Budget Justification 中 PI summer salary ≤ 2 个月（cross-NSF 总和）？
- [ ] 中国合作单位以 collaboration letter 而非 subaward 出现（如非美方主导）？
