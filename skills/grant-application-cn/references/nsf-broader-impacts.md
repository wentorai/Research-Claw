# NSF: Intellectual Merit + Broader Impacts

NSF 所有 proposal 必须明确回答两大评分维度：**Intellectual Merit** 和 **Broader Impacts**。两者**缺一不可**，单独把某一项写得很强但另一项偏弱仍会被退稿。

## 1. NSF Merit Review Criteria（PAPPG 最新版）

每位 reviewer 必须按这两项打分。在 Project Summary 和 Project Description 都需要明确分两个 subsection 标注。

### 1.1 Intellectual Merit

> "The Intellectual Merit criterion encompasses the potential to advance knowledge."

**评审会问的具体问题：**

1. What is the potential for the proposed activity to:
   - Advance knowledge and understanding within its own field?
   - Advance knowledge across different fields?
2. To what extent do the proposed activities suggest and explore creative, original, or potentially transformative concepts?
3. Is the plan well-reasoned, well-organized, and based on a sound rationale?
4. How well qualified is the individual, team, or organization to conduct the proposed activities?
5. Are there adequate resources available to the PI?

### 1.2 Broader Impacts

> "The Broader Impacts criterion encompasses the potential to benefit society and contribute to the achievement of specific, desired societal outcomes."

**NSF 列举的可能 broader impacts 类别：**

- Full participation of women, persons with disabilities, and underrepresented minorities in STEM
- Improved STEM education and educator development at any level
- Increased public scientific literacy and public engagement with science and technology
- Improved well-being of individuals in society
- Development of a diverse, globally competitive STEM workforce
- Increased partnerships between academia, industry, and others
- Improved national security
- Increased economic competitiveness of the United States
- Enhanced infrastructure for research and education

**核心**：必须把 broader impacts 落实到**具体活动 + 受益人群 + 评估指标**，避免空泛。

## 2. Broader Impacts 写作模板

很多 reviewer 抱怨 BI 部分写得"千篇一律 + 不可信"。一份 strong BI 应包含：

```
Broader Impacts

[Anchor activity]: [description] targeting [population] 
through [channel] with [metric].

Specific activities include:

1. K-12 Outreach
   - 与本地高中 / 社区学院的具体合作
   - 每年 X 名学生参与 Y 周课程
   - 评估指标：Z

2. Undergraduate Training
   - REU site / research opportunities
   - 招收人数 + 多样性目标 + 后续 placement 跟踪

3. Open Science / Data Sharing
   - 数据 / 代码 / 教材发布平台与 license
   - 维护承诺 + 估计下载量

4. Industry / Community Partnership
   - 合作单位 + 合作内容 + 知识转移渠道

5. Diversity, Equity, Inclusion
   - 实验室招生政策、mentoring、conference scholarships

Sustainability:
- 项目结束后哪些活动可持续？谁接盘？

Evaluation:
- Internal vs external evaluator
- Formative vs summative evaluation 的 metric 与时间点
```

## 3. Project Summary 的 1 页必备

NSF Project Summary（限 1 页）要求三个 separate sections（必须分别加 header）：

1. **Overview**
2. **Intellectual Merit**
3. **Broader Impacts**

任一 section 缺失或不带 header → **without review 直接退回**。

## 4. CAREER 项目特别要求

NSF CAREER 的 Project Description（15 pages）必须含 **integrated research and education plan**：

- Research plan 与 education plan **不能简单并列**，而是**有机整合**：研究产出（数据 / 工具 / 案例）必须 feed into 教学；教学活动（学生项目 / 课程）反馈进研究
- **Department Chair Letter** 必须支持 PI 的 education plan
- **Department-level integration**：CAREER 体现的是 PI 的"长期职业发展计划"，不只是一个 project

## 5. NSF vs NIH 体例对照

| 维度 | NSF | NIH |
|------|-----|-----|
| 强制 review criteria | Intellectual Merit + Broader Impacts | Significance / Investigator / Innovation / Approach / Environment |
| 1 页摘要 | Project Summary（3 sections） | Specific Aims |
| 主体长度 | Project Description 15 pages | Research Strategy 12 pages |
| 假设驱动？ | 偏好但不强制 | 强制 hypothesis-driven |
| Preliminary data 必需？ | 不强制（CAREER 鼓励） | 强烈建议 |
| Resubmission 制度 | 不允许直接 resubmit；同主题再申请算新 | 可 resubmit 1 次 |

## 6. 评审流程

NSF 通常采用 **panel + ad hoc** 混合：

- Program Officer 分配 3-5 位 ad hoc reviewer 给 written comments
- Panel meeting 集中讨论一批 proposals → 给 ranking（HC / H / M / L / NC）
- Program Officer 综合 ad hoc + panel 给 funding recommendation

无 NIH 式 priority score；funding decision 含 PO 主观裁量空间，因此 PO 沟通在 pre-submission 阶段很重要。

## 7. Data Management & Sharing Plan

NSF 自 2011 年起强制要求 **Data Management Plan (DMP)**，最多 2 页。包含：

- Types of data / samples / physical collections / software / curriculum materials
- Data formats (preferred 开放格式)
- Access policies
- Preservation strategy
- Roles and responsibilities

## 8. 高频退稿原因

1. **Broader Impacts 空泛**：写"我们将增加 underrepresented groups 的参与"但没具体活动 / 指标
2. **Intellectual Merit 与 broader impacts 混淆**：把"产出更多论文"当 broader impact
3. **Project Summary 缺三个 header**
4. **CAREER 的 education plan 写得像后想到的附加**
5. **DMP 缺失或敷衍**
6. **Budget 与 scope 不符**：人员月数 + 设备费配置不合理
7. **Letters of Collaboration 用错语言**：必须是简短"I confirm I'll do X" 风格，不能有评价 / 推荐性内容（PAPPG 明确禁止）
